pranikah - Wedding Preparation App

Plan your wedding easily with pranikah. Manage timeline, budget, and vendors in one free app.

Features

  • Timeline & Checklist - Track every preparation stage from 12 months to 1 week before
  • Budget Management - Set total budget, allocate per category, track actual spending
  • Vendor Directory - Browse and filter wedding vendors by category
  • Priority System - Set task priority (High/Medium/Low) to auto-adjust timeline
  • Multi-language - English and Bahasa Indonesia
  • Multi-currency - IDR, USD, MYR, VND, GBP, EUR, SGD

Persiapan Nikah

Aplikasi persiapan nikah gratis - kelola timeline, budget, vendor, dan checklist pernikahan. Cocok untuk pasangan yang akan menikah.
